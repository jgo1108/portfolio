About

I'm a U.S. Army Captain and MIT graduate engineer with a rare combination of hands-on technical experience, operational leadership, and advanced research credentials. I hold a DoD SMART Scholarship and am completing a Master's in Civil & Environmental Engineering at MIT with a concentration in Topology Optimization and a 5.0 GPA.

Over eight years as an Army officer, I led technical teams, deployed complex network and communications systems across nine international deployments, and managed programs ranging from $2MM equipment integrations to Battalion-wide cloud network implementations.

At MIT, I've extended my technical depth through topology optimization research for additively manufactured parts, hands-on hardware and embedded systems development, and graduate coursework in Agentic AI and operations management.

I thrive in dynamic, fast-moving environments at the intersection of technology and operations — whether coordinating multinational airborne operations or rapidly prototyping a new hardware system from scratch.

Transitioning to industry as a Technical Program Manager or Systems/Mechanical Engineer. Open to defense tech, advanced systems engineering, and innovative hardware and technology companies.
